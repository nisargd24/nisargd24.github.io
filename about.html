The Story

I'm a mechanical engineer with a deep passion for precision — whether it's crafting intricate CAD assemblies in SOLIDWORKS, running turbulent flow simulations in ANSYS Fluent, or architecting smooth, pixel-perfect mobile apps in Flutter.
